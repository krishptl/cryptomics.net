WHY CRYPTOMICS

A governed access layer for biological data.

Secure biodata vault

  • Biological data is stored encrypted by default in a dedicated, access-controlled vault.
  • Modern encryption (e.g., AES-256) and key management to protect data at rest and in transit.
  • A foundation built to support stronger controls and encryption schemes over time.

Controlled records

  • Treat biodata—including genomics—like governed documents, with clear ownership, retention, and sharing rules.
  • Structured records that plug into contracts, protocols, IRB workflows, and audit trails.
  • Integrates with lab, research, and hospital systems instead of replacing them.

Consent & auditability

  • Capture signatures, approvals, and consent decisions for every access request.
  • Maintain a tamper-evident record of who requested, approved, and used which datasets.
  • Support HIPAA/GDPR-aligned workflows with exportable logs and institutional controls.
